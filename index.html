Care Instructions

Follow these to keep the garment at its best for years.

Cold wash

Gentle cycle, inside out, alone.

Mild detergent

No bleach, ever.

No fabric softener

It breaks down the fleece pile.

Tumble dry, low

Low heat only, remove promptly.

Do not iron

Heat can melt synthetic fibers.

Proper storage

Hang, don't fold, long-term.

Pro care tips
  • Turn inside out before washing to reduce pilling on the fleece face
  • Wash with similar colors — polar fleece can pill against rougher fabrics
  • Remove from the dryer promptly to stop static and wrinkling
  • Skip fabric softener and dryer sheets — they flatten the fleece's loft
  • Keep away from Velcro, zippers, or rough surfaces in the wash and in storage
